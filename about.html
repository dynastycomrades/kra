HOW DO OUR SERVICES WORK?

This web based platform works this way:

  1. You to tap the link to our home page.
  2. You fill in the KRA applicaton form and submit the form online.
  3. You will then be requested by one of our agents to send a scan or photo of your id .
  4. Our team will then work behind the scenes to generate your KRA pin.
  5. A screenshot of your KRA certificate is sent to prove it is has been succesfully created.(Pin will be hidden but name will be shown.)
  6. Your KRA pin is sent to your after paying the fee requested.
